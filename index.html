FAQ

  • What is the Lab Equipment Booking Platform?

    The platform allows students and researchers to reserve lab equipment online, ensuring availability and efficient scheduling.

  • How do I access the platform?

    You can log in using your university credentials or the authentication system provided by the institution. For testing purposes, you can try the demo now!

  • How do I book equipment?

    You can browse available equipment, select a time slot, and confirm your booking through the platform. You’ll receive a confirmation notification once it's booked.

  • Can I cancel or modify my booking?

    Yes, you can cancel or change your reservation before the booking start time. Some bookings may have restrictions, so review the cancellation policy before making changes.

  • How do I report a bug or suggest improvements?

    Use the feedback option on the platform or email the support team with details of the issue or suggestion.
